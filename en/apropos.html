ABOUT

Who am I ?

Nice to meet you! I’m Elisa Saltini, a first-year student in the MMI program at Montbéliard. This website was created purely for educational purposes on the following theme: The Matilda Effect.

Why did I make this website ?

The goal was to create a website about a “Matilda”, in other words, a woman forgotten by history in favor of men. We therefore had to write several articles related to the chosen woman.
I personally chose Lucia Moholy because, coming from a design and applied arts school, I have a particular fondness for this culture. It therefore seemed obvious to me to choose a woman from this field.

Any questions ?

If you have any questions for me, don’t hesitate to ask via the “Contact” section, I would be happy to answer them!
