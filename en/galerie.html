MOVIE GALLERY

If the story of Lucia Moholy and the Matilda Effect in general interested you, there are many films exploring similar themes.

BIG EYES,
TIM BURTON

A touching work inspired by the true story of painter Margaret Keane, whose husband claimed authorship of her paintings for years.

Watch trailer
HIDDEN FIGURES,
THEODORE MELFI

A work inspired by true events, focusing on three African-American female mathematicians at NASA who were erased from the official narratives of the space race.

Watch trailer
FRIDA,
JULIE TAYMOR

A moving biopic of Frida Kahlo, long overshadowed by the fame of her husband, the painter Diego Rivera.

Watch trailer
COLETTE,
WASH WESTMORELAND

Tells the story of how novelist Colette had her first books published under her husband Willy’s name before claiming authorship of her work.

Watch trailer
PORTRAIT DE LA JEUNE FILLE EN FEU,
CELINE SCIAMMA

Depicts a painter forced to hide her signature in a society where female artists are erased from recognition.
